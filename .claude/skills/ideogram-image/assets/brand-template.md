# Brand Guidelines for Image Generation

## Brand Identity
- **Name**: Your Company Name
- **Tagline**: Your tagline here

## Visual Style
Describe the overall aesthetic applied to every generated image.

- **Aesthetic**: Modern minimalist with clean lines
- **Medium**: 3D render with soft textures
- **Mood**: Optimistic, professional, approachable
- **Lighting**: Soft natural light, subtle shadows

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep blue (#2563EB) — dominates backgrounds and key elements
- **Secondary**: Emerald green (#10B981) — used for growth/success themes
- **Accent**: Warm amber (#F59E0B) — highlights and call-to-action elements
- **Neutral**: Charcoal (#1F2937) — text and subtle details
- **Background**: Clean white (#FFFFFF) or soft gradients

## Imagery Guidelines

### Include
- Diverse people collaborating naturally
- Clean geometric shapes and patterns
- Subtle tech elements integrated organically
- Open, airy compositions with breathing room

### Avoid
- Cluttered or busy compositions
- Dark, moody, or dystopian atmospheres
- Generic stock photo poses
- Overly saturated or neon colors

## Text in Images
When generating images with text:
- **Font style**: Clean sans-serif appearance
- **Placement**: Centered or lower-third, never obscuring subjects
- **Keep text short**: 1-5 words performs best

## Default Settings
- **Aspect ratio**: 16:9 (hero), 1:1 (social), 9:16 (stories)
- **Style type**: AUTO
- **Rendering speed**: DEFAULT
