*  #### What does a website cost with you? 

  
  You won't get an off-the-shelf website from us, but a sound investment in your digital future. Transparency is the basis of our partnership.
  
  *  **Efficient starter solutions:** Our web design packages start at CHF 4,500 for start-ups and clear projects that require fast and professional implementation.
  
  *  **Individual, strategic web design:** For comprehensive projects that require an in-depth strategy, a unique design and individual functions, investments start at CHF 10,000.
  
  You will receive a clear fixed price offer from us for every project. This gives you full cost control and planning security right from the start.
  

*  #### How long does it take to develop a website? 

  
  Our process is designed for efficiency and the highest quality. We realize most website projects within **8 to 10 weeks** from strategic kick-off to go-live. But if it has to be done quickly, we can also do it [in 7 days](https://noevu.ch/en/blog/squarespace-webseite-fuer-ferienwohnung).
  
  For you, this means that we don't invest our energy in endless technical setups, but rather, thanks to the smart use of Squarespace, directly in what counts: Your strategy, your message and your design. The key to rapid progress lies in your decisiveness and our proven, clear process.
  

*  #### Can you also help us with the texts? 

  
  Absolutely. A clear message is at the heart of every successful website. If you don't have the words, we'll step in.
  
  You provide us with your expert knowledge in the form of keywords and conversations. We transform this essence into **SEO-optimized content** that not only informs your target group, but also convinces them. This is a central part of our **website consulting**.
  

*  #### We lack professional images. What now? 

  
  The visual design is crucial for the first impression and the emotional impact of your brand. We won't leave you alone here either.
  
  Together we will find the perfect solution for you:
  
  *  **AI-supported visual worlds:** We create unique and consistent visual worlds that visually emphasize your uniqueness.
  
  *  **Professional photography:** We connect you with experienced photographers from our network.
  
  *  **Curated stock images:** We research and license high-quality images that fit your brand.

Here you can dynamically select various options and modules and see transparently how your decisions affect the overall investment. We discuss this offer together to put together the perfect package for you.

This is immediately followed by the most important part: the joint preparation workshop. This is where we dive deep into the details, define the user journey, sharpen the core messages and lay the strategic foundation for the entire project.
  
  For you, this means a transparent, comprehensible process in which you are actively involved from the outset and have the certainty that your project is based on a solid, strategic foundation.
