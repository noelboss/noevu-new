## The most frequently asked questions about website advice and help for Swiss SMEs.


*  #### How does support work for existing websites? 

  
  We offer targeted help with technical, content-related or strategic issues relating to your existing website. Whether performance problems, design optimization or integration of new functions: Our experts analyze the situation and work with you to implement the appropriate measures - efficiently, comprehensibly and always with your goals in mind.
  

*  #### Will you also take over the ongoing maintenance of our website? 

  
  Yes, we take care of updates, security, content adjustments and technical optimizations. With our support, your website will always be up-to-date, efficient and secure - so that you can concentrate on your core business.
  

*  #### Do you also advise on Squarespace-specific topics? 

  
  As a Swiss Squarespace Gold Partner, we advise you on all questions relating to Squarespace - from selection and setup to optimization and expansion. We will show you how to use the full potential of the platform and how to easily manage your website yourself.
  

*  #### Can we also get help with acute problems at short notice? 

  
  Of course. If you have urgent concerns or technical problems, we'll be on hand to help you quickly and easily. Simply contact us - we will quickly find a solution to ensure that your website runs reliably.


*  #### How does Noevu help optimize our existing website? 

  
  We analyze your current website, identify weaknesses and point out specific opportunities for improvement - from user guidance and design to SEO and performance. You benefit from clear recommendations and practical implementation to make your website even more successful.
  

*  #### What makes Noevu's Squarespace consulting different from other providers? 

  
  You won't receive standard solutions, but individual advice based on your goals and requirements. We combine many years of experience with in-depth Squarespace know-how and accompany you from strategy to implementation - comprehensibly, transparently and always at eye level.
  

*  #### Can we also get training for the CMS from you? 

  
  Yes, we offer practical training for Squarespace and other CMS - individually tailored to your team. You will learn how to manage content securely and efficiently yourself and how to make the most of your website.
  

*  #### Does Noevu also support migration from other systems to Squarespace? 

  
  We are happy to handle the entire migration, whether from WordPress, Typo3 or another system. We plan the migration strategically, ensure a smooth process and make sure that SEO rankings and important content are retained.
