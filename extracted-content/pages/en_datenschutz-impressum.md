### 1\. categories of personal data that we process

We process the following categories of personal data:


*  Master data: Personal information that we require to conduct our business relationships, including name, date of birth, postal address, telephone number, e-mail address, job function and position, and consent forms.
  

*  Application Data: Personal information submitted or disclosed by you as part of job eligibility checks or during the application process, including application documents and information related to the application process.
  

*  Identification data: Personal information required for access controls and to create a user account, including username and password.
  

*  Transaction data: Personal information that arises in the context of the conclusion or execution of a contract, including information about the application process and contract administration.
  

*  Financial data: Personal information that arises in the course of the financial management of our contractual or other business relationships, including billing and payment information.
  

*  Technical data: Personal information generated when you use our website or other electronic offerings, including IP address, operating system information, date, time and duration of use, browser type, reference URL and log-in data.
  

*  Preference Data: Personal information that is generated as we try to get to know you better and better target our products, services and the Website to you through appropriate analysis and evaluation, including information about your responses to electronic communications, location data and interactions with our social media profiles.
  
We receive the majority of this personal data directly from you. To the extent permitted by law, we also obtain personal data from publicly accessible sources or receive it from authorities and other third parties, for example contractual partners or internet analysis services.


### 2\. purposes of processing your personal data

We process your personal data for the following purposes:


*  Business relationship: We enter into, manage and process contractual relationships.
  

*  Application procedure: We carry out aptitude tests and handle the application process, including the associated documentation.
  

*  Advertising and marketing: We organize events and send you personalized advertising about our offers via all contact channels known to us (this includes newsletters and other regular contacts as well as targeted marketing campaigns such as competitions, prize draws or similar events).
  

*  Market and opinion research: We analyze and evaluate the use of our products, services and website, further develop our offerings and conduct opinion surveys.
  

*  Security and access control: We monitor and control our networks and IT infrastructures, carry out system and error checks, document these processes, create backup copies and operate monitoring systems.
  

*  Legal obligations and compliance: We carry out KYC (Know Your Customer) checks, comply with disclosure, information and reporting obligations, archive data in accordance with legal requirements and implement measures to prevent, detect and investigate criminal offenses.
  

*  Legal claims: We are active in the assertion, enforcement and defense of legal claims.
  

*  Operation and maintenance of our website: We take care of the maintenance, provision, improvement and monitoring of our products, services and website.
  
These data processing operations are essential for the management of our business relationships and the provision of our services. They enable us to continuously improve our offerings and ensure your security and compliance with legal requirements.


### 3\. legal bases of data processing

We process your personal data on the basis of the following legal bases:


*  Consent: If we process your personal data on the basis of your consent, you will be specifically informed about the purposes of the data processing. You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time. Withdrawal of consent is free of charge and can be done without giving reasons. The withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.
  

*  Contract processing: The processing of your personal data is necessary for the initiation or processing of contractual relationships.
  

*  Legitimate interests: Processing is necessary for the purposes of the legitimate interests pursued by us or by a third party, except where such interests are overridden by your interests or fundamental rights and freedoms which require protection of personal data.
  

*  Legal obligations: The processing of your data is necessary to fulfill legal obligations to which we are subject.
  
These diverse legal bases enable us to act appropriately and in accordance with the law in various situations in order to ensure an efficient and secure service. In doing so, we always ensure that your rights and freedoms are respected and protected.
