*  #### Is AI also affordable for small Swiss companies? 

  
  Yes, absolutely. Many AI solutions today are scalable and can also be implemented with smaller budgets. We emphasize cost-efficient approaches and show you how you can achieve a high return on investment with targeted AI investments.
  

*  #### How will AI affect jobs in my SME? 

  
  AI is not a replacement for your employees, but a powerful tool that supports them. AI automates repetitive tasks, allowing your team to focus on more creative and strategic activities. We support you with the integration and offer training to make your employees fit for the future of AI.
