## Resource-saving web design for non-profit organizations


#### For non-profit organizations, a strong digital presence is not just a business card, but a crucial tool to spread their mission and mobilize support. As Swiss Squarespace experts, we were able to accompany the "Future for Kibambili" association on its journey. The challenge was to create a professional, effective and easy-to-maintain website with limited resources. We'll show you how we used strategic website consulting and targeted website development to create a digital platform that not only informs, but also inspires and motivates people to take action.


### Project: Squarespace web design for "Future for Kibambili"

The "Future for Kibambili" association does indispensable work in the Democratic Republic of Congo. In order to make this work visible and secure financial support, a professional and trustworthy online presence was essential.


* * *


### Customer


*  **Swiss NGO / non-profit association "** [**Future for Kibambili**](http://kibambili.com/) **"**
  

*  Focus: Promoting education, healthcare and economic development
  

*  Target group: Donors, members and interested parties in Switzerland
  

*  Mission: Creating prospects for the future in Kibambili, DR Congo


### Project


*  Complete relaunch of the outdated website
  

*  Development of a clear content strategy according to the Golden Circle principle
  

*  Seamless integration of donation and newsletter functions
  

*  Conception of an emotionally appealing and authentic visual world
  

*  Training of the volunteer team for independent content management


### Special features


*  Working with a volunteer team with limited technical knowledge.
  

*  Requirement for a maximum cost-efficient solution without compromising on quality.
  

*  The need to communicate complex projects and their impact in a simple and emotionally understandable way.


* * *


## The solution: Strategic web design & development

!\[Image\](file:///Users/Noel\_1/Development/repos/noevu-new-2/extracted-content/old-site-backup/html/images/43b03ec1-a29c-47ac-af0e-6520f962e9ee/6b2f68439dc36dbe0c677a2320ebbdf44bada3b9fd19b1e97d99be8b15bf4216.png)

Squarespace impressed with its powerful content management, blog, donation and membership tools, which made it possible to quickly realize a high-quality, fully equipped website without complex development requirements. With Squarespace, blog posts can be sent directly as newsletters. Analytics included. This saves time, money and nerves.

As an experienced **Squarespace partner in Switzerland**, our approach went far beyond pure design. We defined the strategic goals together in an initial kick-off workshop. The entire project was handled transparently via an interactive project website, which enabled an efficient feedback loop.

We reworked the existing content and created a clear, compelling narrative around the association's vision and specific projects. Instead of resorting to expensive photo shoots, we curated an atmospheric image concept from high-quality stock shots.

Squarespace was chosen because the platform offers a powerful all-in-one solution: From an intuitive content management system to integrated blog, donation and email marketing tools to robust analytics features. This enabled us to create a first-class website for the association, which the team can now manage completely independently after a short training course.

**Implemented services:**


*  Strategic **website consulting** for NGOs
  

*  Concept and **web design** based on Squarespace
  

*  Professional **Squarespace website development**
  

*  Creation of **SEO-optimized content** (text and images)
  

*  Integration of donation tools and e-mail marketing (Squarespace Campaigns)
  

*  Launch support and comprehensive Squarespace training
  

*  Ongoing technical maintenance and strategic optimization

## The result

The new website of "Future for Kibambili" is not only visually appealing, but also functionally superior. The fresh, clear design creates trust and reflects the professionalism of the association. Visitors quickly find their way around thanks to the intuitive structure and can donate or subscribe to the newsletter with just a few clicks. The images we integrated are not only aesthetically pleasing, but also feature SEO-optimized alt texts to further increase online visibility.
