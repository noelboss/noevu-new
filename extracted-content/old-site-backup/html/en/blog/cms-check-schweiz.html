What is a CMS check?

A CMS check (also known as a CMS test, CMS comparison, CMS selection or CMS evaluation) is a short, structured comparison of relevant criteria (costs, security, maintenance, range of functions, operation) with a specific recommendation for your company - based on Swiss SME practice.

How can we find the best CMS for our company?

In today's digital landscape, a professional online presence is essential for every Swiss SME, organization and business. But choosing the right content management system (CMS) - the software behind your website - can be a complex and often overwhelming task.

With so many options available, from flexible open source solutions to integrated all-in-one platforms, you face the challenge of finding the system that is not only technically convincing, but also perfectly suited to your business goals, resources and working methods.

Making the wrong decision when choosing a CMS can have far-reaching consequences: unnecessary costs, technical hurdles, limited scalability and ultimately an online presence that does not reach its full potential. This is precisely where our free online CMS evaluation tool for Swiss SMEs comes in.

Based on over 25 years of experience in web development, we have created an intuitive tool to help you make the strategically wiser decision between the two alternative systems for Swiss companies - WordPress and Squarespace. Our goal is to provide you with a sound decision-making tool so that you can invest in a digital solution that will really drive your business forward and ensure your long-term success.

There are two popular CMS alternatives to choose from: Wordpress or Squarespace

On one side of the CMS check is WordPress: the open, infinitely flexible modular system that attracts users as "free" software. It is a gigantic marketplace with tens of thousands of plugins that promises unlimited possibilities, but also shifts full responsibility for technology, security, and maintenance onto you. The best alternative to WordPress in 2026 is Squarespace: the integrated all-in-one platform designed for maximum efficiency, security, and professional results without technical ballast. A carefully curated ecosystem where everything comes from a single source.

Which CMS is best suited to the goals of your Swiss SME?

Choosing a content management system is much more than a purely technical matter; it is a strategic decision for the digital future of your company. A CMS is the foundation of your online presence and should optimally support your business goals. Before you decide on WordPress or Squarespace, it is crucial to define your specific requirements and the role your website should play in your business model.

Analyze your business goals: What should the website achieve?

Ask yourself the following questions: Should your website primarily serve as a digital business card to provide information? Are you planning to sell products or services directly online (e-commerce)? Is a blog of central importance for content marketing? Do you need special functions such as appointment booking systems, member areas or complex forms? The answers to these questions will help you to determine the range of functions that your CMS must offer.

Resources and know-how: How much time and budget can you invest?

Another critical factor is your internal resources. Do you have employees with technical know-how who can take care of the maintenance, security and further development of the website? Or do you prefer a solution that largely relieves you of these tasks so that you can concentrate on your core business? Consider not only the initial costs of creating the website, but also ongoing expenses for hosting, premium themes/plugins, security solutions and potential extensions. These considerations are particularly relevant for Swiss SMEs, which often operate with limited budgets and human resources.

WordPress vs Squarespace: A detailed comparison for Swiss companies

The choice between WordPress and Squarespace is often a decision between maximum flexibility and maximum efficiency. Both systems have their justification and their specific strengths that make them attractive for different use cases. For Swiss SMEs, it is crucial to understand the respective philosophies and practical implications in order to make an informed decision.

WordPress: The flexible open source solution for SMEs in Switzerland

WordPress is the world's most widely used content management system and is known for its almost limitless customizability. As open source software, it offers you enormous freedom in terms of design and functionality. Thousands of themes and plugins make it possible to implement almost any conceivable function - from complex e-commerce solutions to specialized booking systems. The large community and the wealth of online resources are further advantages that make WordPress attractive.

Advantages of WordPress for Swiss SMEs:

  • Unlimited customizability: WordPress can be adapted exactly to your requirements using themes and plugins.

  • Huge variety of plugins: There is a suitable plugin for almost every function, often free or inexpensive.

  • Large community and support: A huge global community provides support and resources.

  • Full control: You have full control over your data and the server on which your website is hosted.

Disadvantages of WordPress for Swiss SMEs:

  • High maintenance effort: Regular updates of core, themes and plugins are essential for security and functionality.

  • Security risks: Due to its popularity, WordPress is a frequent target for hacker attacks, which requires constant vigilance.

  • Technical complexity: For non-technical people, managing and troubleshooting WordPress can quickly become overwhelming.

  • Hidden costs: Although the software is free, there are costs for hosting, premium themes/plugins, security solutions and professional maintenance, which can add up.

When WordPress makes sense for Swiss SMEs:

WordPress is the right choice if you are a Swiss SME in need of a very specific, highly customized website that goes beyond standard features. If you have in-house technical resources or are willing to invest in professional maintenance and development to take advantage of full control and flexibility, WordPress can be a powerful platform. It is particularly suitable for companies that are pursuing a long-term, customized digital strategy and are willing to bear the associated effort.

Squarespace: The all-in-one platform for efficient Swiss websites

Squarespace, on the other hand, takes an integrated approach. It is an all-in-one platform that bundles hosting, design, content management and support into one package. This greatly simplifies management and makes Squarespace a popular choice for businesses like yours who want a professional website without the technical baggage. The platform is known for its aesthetically pleasing templates and intuitive user interface, allowing even beginners to create stunning websites.

Advantages of Squarespace for Swiss SMEs:

  • Ease of use: The intuitive drag-and-drop interface makes creating and maintaining your website extremely easy.

  • Integrated security and maintenance: Squarespace takes care of all technical aspects such as hosting, updates and security, so you can focus on your business.

  • Professional design: High-quality, responsive templates ensure a modern and appealing appearance on all devices.

  • Excellent support: Fast and competent customer service helps with questions and problems.

  • Transparent costs: A monthly or annual subscription covers all essential services, with no hidden additional costs.

Disadvantages of Squarespace for Swiss SMEs:

  • Less flexibility for in-depth customizations: Compared to WordPress, the customization options are limited, especially for very specific functions that are not natively supported.

  • Monthly costs: The platform is subscription-based, which means ongoing costs, even if these are transparent and plannable - and are usually even lower compared to hosting Wordpress + plugins.

When Squarespace is the smarter choice for Swiss SMEs:

Squarespace is ideal for Swiss SMEs like you who need a professional, aesthetically pleasing and low-maintenance website. If you value ease of use, built-in security and reliable support and want to focus on your core business, Squarespace is often the strategically wiser choice. It is ideal for service providers, small online stores, portfolios and blogs that want to get online quickly and efficiently without having to worry about technical details.

What are the common misconceptions when choosing a CMS?

When choosing the right content management system, there are many myths and half-truths circulating that can lead to wrong decisions, especially for Swiss SMEs. It is important to debunk these misconceptions in order to create realistic expectations and find the best solution for your individual needs.

Myth "WordPress is free": The true costs of hosting, plugins and maintenance.

One of the most persistent misconceptions is that WordPress is a "free" solution. It is true that the basic WordPress software is open source and can be downloaded free of charge. But a functioning and secure WordPress website requires far more than just the software itself. You need hosting, a domain, often paid premium themes and plugins for extended functions, as well as regular maintenance, security updates and backups. These running costs and the time required for maintenance can quickly add up and negate the supposed cost savings. For many Swiss SMEs that do not have internal IT resources, this often means that they have to hire external service providers, which incurs additional costs.

"Squarespace is only for creatives": versatility for different sectors.

Another misconception is that Squarespace is primarily for creatives such as photographers or designers. While Squarespace undoubtedly has outstanding design tools and aesthetically pleasing templates, the platform is far more versatile than many assume. It offers robust features for e-commerce, blogging, appointment booking and portfolios that are relevant to a wide range of industries and business types. Many Swiss SMEs from the service sector, trade or commerce find Squarespace a powerful yet easy-to-manage solution that meets their professional needs.

"I need maximum flexibility": when too much flexibility becomes a burden

It is often assumed that "maximum flexibility" is always best. WordPress does indeed offer almost unlimited customizability. But this flexibility comes at a price: it requires a higher level of technical knowledge, more time for configuration and maintenance and an increased risk of compatibility issues between different plugins and themes. For many Swiss SMEs that want to concentrate on their core business and do not have their own web development department, this "maximum flexibility" can quickly become a burden. An integrated solution such as Squarespace, which offers fewer customization options but guarantees stability, security and ease of use, is in many cases the more pragmatic and efficient choice.

Conclusion: Finding the right CMS is individual

Choosing the right content management system is one of the most important strategic decisions for the digital success of your Swiss SME. It not only influences the functionality and appearance of your website, but also the effort required to maintain it, its security and ultimately your ability to be successful online.
